Creative Culture Global Summit 2015 Logo

The purpose of a logo competition which was held from June 8th to July 7th was to gather a variety of design concepts from different designers and select the best one to represent CC Global Summit 2015. The competition organizer set the requirements, guidelines, and timeline for the competition, including any specific elements or themes that the logo should incorporate.
